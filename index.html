Welcome

Located in the heart of Greensboro, where coffee and community come together.

At Union Coffee, our focus is on you—because you matter. We believe that everything starts with people, and our mission is to reflect that care in every detail of what we do. From the moment you walk through our doors, we strive to create a truly unique and memorable experience tailored just for you.

Our passion extends beyond the café itself to the quality and craftsmanship of every cup we serve. We are deeply committed to honoring the many hands involved in bringing coffee from farm to cup, ensuring that each step of the process is guided by respect, integrity, and excellence.

We invite you to visit and experience the difference for yourself. It would be our pleasure to serve you.

Proudly Partnering with
Night Swim Coffee

At Union Coffee, we are proud to feature Night Swim Coffee as our primary wholesale partner. Their dedication to quality and sustainability aligns perfectly with our mission to deliver exceptional coffee experiences.

To keep things fresh and exciting, we also cycle through a variety of roasters, offering you the chance to explore a diverse range of flavors and styles.
